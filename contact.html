A Note on Grant Applications

The Christenson Family Foundation does not accept unsolicited grant applications. Our grantmaking is invitation-only, guided each year by the priorities and passions of our family board.

We are grateful for the extraordinary work happening across so many organizations, and we recognize this means we are unable to fund many worthy causes. We appreciate your understanding.

If you have a question or a genuine connection to the foundation, please don't hesitate to reach out by email.
