Our Mission

Grateful for our blessings.
Committed to sharing them.

The Christenson Family Foundation was born from a simple, deeply held conviction: those of us who have been fortunate have a responsibility to help others find their footing. Since our first grants in 2006, we've worked to support organizations that teach, heal, and inspire — touching mind, body, and spirit.

We follow our values — and the passion of our family board — to find where our giving can matter most.
